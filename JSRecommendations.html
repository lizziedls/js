Terrifier.

Year of release: 2016.
Genre: Horror/Criminal.

On Halloween night, a maniacal clown terrorizes three girls, as well as anyone else who gets in his way.

Sinister.

Year of release: 2012.
Genre: Horror/Detective.

Together with his family, the author of detectives settles in a small town, in the house, where almost a year ago unfolded a chilling tragedy - all the tenants were killed. The writer accidentally finds video footage, which is the key to the mystery of the crime. But nothing is given in vain: in the house begin to happen terrible things and now the lives of his loved ones are at risk. They are faced with something from which there is no escape....

Curse.

Year of release: 2002.
Genre: Horror/Detective.

Many years ago, tragedy struck this house: A man killed his wife, and the fate of their six-year-old son Toshio is unknown, the boy disappeared. Dying, the unhappy woman cursed the murderer and the place where she met her death. Now a fierce ghost brings death to all who dare to approach his domain.

Insidious.

Year of release: 2010.
Genre: Horror/Detective.

Josh and Renee move into a new home with their children, but no sooner have they unpacked than strange events begin. Inexplicably moving objects, strange sounds in the nursery, but the real nightmare begins when their ten-year-old son Dalton falls into a coma. All the doctors' efforts to help the boy are unsuccessful.

Hereditary.

Year of release: 2018.
Genre: Horror/Detective.

After the death of Annie Graham's mother, something inexplicable begins to happen in her previously peaceful home. Now the lives of her loved ones are at risk. With each passing day, more of their family's horrifying secrets are revealed.
